Short Grit Scale

Measure your perseverance and passion for long-term goals. Grit reflects sustained effort and consistent interest over time — the stamina to keep going when things get hard.

8 questions · ~2 minutes · Developed by Angela Duckworth & Patrick Quinn (2009). Two subscales: Consistency of Interest + Perseverance of Effort.

For informational and research purposes. Not a clinical diagnostic tool.
